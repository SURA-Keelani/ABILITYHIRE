Help & Support

Welcome to the AbilityHire help page. Here you can find answers to common questions and guidance on how to use the platform.

Voice Navigation

Use the voice navigation button at the bottom right to navigate the site using your voice. Say commands like "Home", "Login", "Search Job", "User Training", etc.

Forget Password

If you forgot your password, use the "Forget Password" link on the login page to reset it via your registered email.

User Training

Access the user training page to learn how to use the platform effectively.

AI Matching

Our AI-powered job matching helps you find the best job opportunities based on your profile and preferences.

Accessibility

We support screen readers and provide ARIA roles for better accessibility. Use voice commands for easier navigation.

Contact Support

If you need further assistance, please contact our support team at support@abilityhire.com.
